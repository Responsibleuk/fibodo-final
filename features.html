Client Booking Management

The only fully integrated booking self-management in golf FIBODO empowers your clients to be able to book amend and manage their bookings when and where they want, within the booking terms you decide are applicable. No more frustrated telephone from anxious clients trying to amend their bookings, no more ambiguity for you or your team on how to manage booking amendments or cancellations and no more long-winded processing of manual cancellation refunds, as well as reducing the risk of theft within the business. FIBODO also provides walk-in booking functionality.
special offer

Real-time channel management

FIBODO instantly distributes all your inventory on line, offering your clients the opportunity to book your services in any combination you want them to, at a time convenient to them. FIBODO’s seamless sales promotion tools, allow you to target selected audiences with booking incentives using business defined promotion codes, whilst maximising revenues from those customers whose priority is time over price. Using your tailored FIBODO App means that you have the opportunity to build relationships with all you known clients, targeting them to drive your business.

Live Revenue Management

FIBODO revenue management provides businesses with the opportunity to define their pricing strategy based on product profitability, automatically adjusting product availability based on business demand. This means that you can be sure you are always selling your products at the optimal price and in accordance with your strategic objectives, whether you are in the business or on holidays half way around the globe.

Reporting

FIBODO Reporting is included with all modules and provides unparalleled reporting on your business performance. Reports are provided in multiple formats, from simple dashboard, to detailed numerical content. Reports automatically export to CSV files making them readily accessible to main stream accounting and analytics systems. Sales reporting provides for segmentation, date ranging, including combining future and past bookings within the same report. FIBODO reports are consistent with each other and accurate, so you can rely on them to inform your business strategy

Tailored App

FIBODO provides application platforms for both IOS and Android that seamlessly integrate into your client management system, allowing you to create your own full integrated client ecosystem. The functionality of the App makes your clients booking journey easier, improves their engagement with your services and experiences (live content and direct in App messaging) and always ensures you are with them at all times, as their preferred partner and service provider.

Multi Activity Packaging

FIBODO is designed to allow customers to build their own activity planners. Once you make your inventory, courses or equipment available to purchase, your customers can build their own tailored packages. You can also develop your own product packaging, with full real time equipment inventory and facility control, making sure that shared facilities are not double booked and that you have the necessary equipment and space available to perform the activity booked.

Wallet Manager

FIBODO wallet manager offers you complete control over your finances and for those operating with contractors, such as golf professionals, complete control over how your contractors are paid. This includes dealing with the processing so that your professionals are not unnecessarily subject to VAT just because they are working within your academy structure. You automatically paid for services delivered and there is no need for burdensome administration to ensure your accounts are in order. The wallet systems means that in the event a coach moves on the wallet set up, means undelivered activities can be assigned to a new coach with all the funds in place to support their delivery.

Social ranking and client communications

Your FIBIODO system is automatically integrated into TRUSTPILOT, one of the most-recognised review platforms on the web, providing you with immediate direct feedback from real paying consumers and boosting your businesses web ranking in the process. All users customers can be set to receive post experience review requests, which can also trigger payment processing, which reduces the risk of unresolved service issues and resultant chargebacks, protecting your income.

Courses and multi experience packages

FIBODO is designed to manage course sales, programming and administration. You simply set up your course or group of events on your FIBODO platform, with either a pre-defined schedule, or allow your clients freedom to book sessions as they go and the system does the rest. Your clients will receive automatic notifications in advance of their sessions, reducing no-shows and allowing them to amend their attendance within the terms and conditions you have agreed. The system will recognise the sessions booked on your clients’ accounts, reducing your liability and ensuring they are informed, avoiding unnecessary administration, or indeed disputes arising out of communication breakdowns. FIBODO will also prompt you if your clients have not responded to engagement emails, ensuring you can take targeted action to re-engage them. Finally the FIBODO wallet system ensures you get paid without the need to chase clients, issue invoices and receipts, making accounting and administration simple and easy.

Market Place

The unique FIBODO design allows the creation of Enterprise and Industry marketplaces, allowing you to present all your activities at multiple locations to your client-base, or to share your inventory across any selected market-place. FIBODO have recently developed the UK golf player App a new market place for golf, supported by the major UK industry stakeholders and governing bodies, that provides an alternative distribution channel for golf courses, academies and ranges with much lower transaction costs than the traditional tee sheet resellers that have been so destructive within the industry over the past ten years.
